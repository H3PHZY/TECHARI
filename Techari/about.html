Our Purpose

Techari is designed to address the lack of accessible mentorship opportunities by connecting aspiring women in tech with experienced mentors. Our platform bridges the mentorship gap, supporting efforts to increase diversity and foster inclusivity in the tech industry.

Through innovative features and personalized matching, we create meaningful connections that drive career growth and build lasting professional relationships.

Key Features

  • Profile Matching: Advanced algorithms to connect mentees with compatible mentors
  • Mentorship Request Flows: Streamlined process for requesting and managing mentorship sessions
  • Session Tracking: Comprehensive tools to monitor progress and growth
  • Community Building: Foster connections beyond one-on-one mentorship

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between men and women in the tech industry by providing a dedicated platform that enables:

  • Skill development through expert guidance
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Confidence building in tech environments
  • Innovation across Africa and beyond

Platform Vision

Techari is a standalone mentorship platform that functions independently to deliver streamlined mentorship matching and session management. Unlike other platforms, we focus specifically on women in tech, creating a safe and supportive environment for:

  • Personalized growth journeys
  • Meaningful professional connections
  • Skill development and career guidance
  • Community support and networking
